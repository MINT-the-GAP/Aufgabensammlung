__$a)\;\;$__ $  \dfrac{7}{8} - \dfrac{1}{4} = $ [[  5/8  ]] @canvas
@Algebrite.check(5/8)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
\dfrac{7}{8} - \dfrac{1}{4} &= \dfrac{7}{8} - \dfrac{1 \cdot 2}{4 \cdot 2} \\
&= \dfrac{7}{8} - \dfrac{2}{8} \\
&= \dfrac{7-2}{8} \\
&= \dfrac{5}{8}
\end{align*}
$$
