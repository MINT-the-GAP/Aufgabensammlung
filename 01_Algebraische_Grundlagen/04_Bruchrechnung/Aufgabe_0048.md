__$a)\;\;$__ $  \dfrac{1}{3} + \dfrac{1}{4} + \dfrac{1}{6} = $ [[  3/4  ]] @canvas
@Algebrite.check(3/4)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
\dfrac{1}{3} + \dfrac{1}{4} + \dfrac{1}{6}
&= \dfrac{1\cdot 4}{3\cdot 4} + \dfrac{1\cdot 3}{4\cdot 3} + \dfrac{1\cdot 2}{6\cdot 2} \\
&= \dfrac{4}{12} + \dfrac{3}{12} + \dfrac{2}{12} \\
&= \dfrac{4+3+2}{12} \\
&= \dfrac{9}{12} \\
&= \dfrac{3}{4}
\end{align*}
$$
************
