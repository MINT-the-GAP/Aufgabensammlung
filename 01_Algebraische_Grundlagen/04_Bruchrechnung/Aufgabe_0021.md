__$a)\;\;$__ $  2 - \dfrac{3}{5} = $ [[  7/5  ]] @canvas
@Algebrite.check(7/5)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
2 - \dfrac{3}{5} &= \dfrac{2 \cdot 5}{1 \cdot 5} - \dfrac{3}{5} \\
&= \dfrac{10}{5} - \dfrac{3}{5} \\
&= \dfrac{10-3}{5} \\
&= \dfrac{7}{5}
\end{align*}
$$
************
