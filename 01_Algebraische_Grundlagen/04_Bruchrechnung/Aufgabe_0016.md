__$a)\;\;$__ $  7 + \dfrac{3}{10} = $ [[  73/10  ]]
@Algebrite.check(73/10)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
7 + \dfrac{3}{10} &= \dfrac{7 \cdot 10}{1 \cdot 10} + \dfrac{3}{10} \\
&= \dfrac{70}{10} + \dfrac{3}{10} \\
&= \dfrac{70+3}{10} \\
&= \dfrac{73}{10}
\end{align*}
$$
************
