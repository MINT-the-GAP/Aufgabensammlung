__$a)\;\;$__ $  \dfrac{13}{18} - \dfrac{1}{9} = $ [[  11/18  ]] @canvas
@Algebrite.check(11/18)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
\dfrac{13}{18} - \dfrac{1}{9} &= \dfrac{13}{18} - \dfrac{1 \cdot 2}{9 \cdot 2} \\
&= \dfrac{13}{18} - \dfrac{2}{18} \\
&= \dfrac{13-2}{18} \\
&= \dfrac{11}{18}
\end{align*}
$$
************
