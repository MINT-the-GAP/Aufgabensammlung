# Addition von Brüchen

__$a)\;\;$__ $  \dfrac{3}{14} + \dfrac{1}{7} = $ [[  5/14  ]]
@Algebrite.check(5/14)
************
$$
\begin{align*}
\dfrac{3}{14} + \dfrac{1}{7} &= \dfrac{3}{14} + \dfrac{1 \cdot 2}{7 \cdot 2} \\
&= \dfrac{3}{14} + \dfrac{2}{14} \\
&= \dfrac{5}{14}
\end{align*}
$$
************
